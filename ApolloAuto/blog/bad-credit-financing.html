Bad Credit Used Car Financing in Ventura & LA Counties

Used car financing · Simi Valley & El Monte · Apollo Auto

A low credit score doesn't mean you're stuck without a used car in Ventura County or Los Angeles County. Every week we talk to buyers at our Simi Valley and El Monte lots who got turned down somewhere else, or who figured they wouldn't qualify at all. Here's what lenders actually care about, and what you can do before you walk in.

What lenders look at for used car financing (besides the score)

  • Income and stability. Can you afford the payment? Bring proof of income if you can.
  • Down payment. Even a little money down can help you get approved and lower your monthly payment.
  • Time on the job. Steady work matters, especially if you're buying your first car.
  • Recent credit history. Bankruptcy, repossession, or late payments don't automatically mean no. Timing and how you've recovered since then matter.

Before you apply for bad credit auto financing

  1. Figure out your real budget: payment, insurance, gas, and maintenance, not just the price on the windshield.
  2. Bring pay stubs or proof of income and a valid ID.
  3. Think about a trade-in. It can lower how much you need to finance.
  4. Be straight with us about your situation. That helps us find the right program for you.

Used car purchase paperwork overview

Every deal is different, but most purchases include documents like these. Tim will walk you through yours line by line. This is a general overview, not legal advice.

Buyer's order / purchase agreement
The summary of the vehicle, price, trade-in, fees, and total. Review every number before you move forward.
Retail installment contract
Your financing agreement if you are making payments. Shows amount financed, APR, payment amount, and term length.
Buyer's Guide (FTC)
Discloses whether the vehicle is sold "as-is" or with a warranty. Read it before you buy.
Odometer statement
Records the mileage at sale. Federal requirement on most used vehicle sales.
Title & registration paperwork
Transfers ownership and gets the car registered in your name. We help coordinate this part of the process.

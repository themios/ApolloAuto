Simi Valley used car FAQ

How do I browse used cars at your Simi Valley lot?

Shop online at apolloauto-to.com for current Simi Valley inventory, or call (805) 404-3873. Tell Tim what you're looking for — budget, body style, or a specific listing — and he'll point you to the right vehicles and confirm availability.

Can I get pre-approved before visiting Simi Valley?

Yes. Call or message Tim with basic information about your situation — credit history, income, what you're looking to spend. He can give you a realistic picture of what you may qualify for before you make the trip to Simi Town Center.

Does the Simi Valley lot accept trade-ins?

Yes. Trade-in value can reduce your down payment at the Simi Valley location. See our trade-in tips guide for what factors affect value, or mention your current vehicle when you reach out and Tim can give you a ballpark.

What types of used vehicles does the Simi Valley lot carry?

The Simi Valley inventory typically includes used sedans, used SUVs and crossovers, used trucks, and used minivans across a range of price points. Inventory rotates regularly — check apolloauto-to.com for current listings and prices.

Is the pricing at Simi Valley negotiable? Are there hidden fees?

Apollo Auto uses transparent pricing. The price you agree to is the price you pay — no surprise documentation charges, no add-on fees at signing, no last-minute line items. You can review redacted contract examples before you visit so there are no surprises.

What do I need to bring to buy a used car in Simi Valley?

Bring a valid California driver's license or ID, proof of insurance (required before you drive off the lot in California), and proof of income if you're financing. If you're trading in a vehicle, bring the title. Our full checklist covers everything.

How far is the Simi Valley lot from Thousand Oaks?

Apollo Auto Simi Valley is approximately 15–20 minutes from Thousand Oaks via the 23 Freeway or surface streets, and similarly accessible from Moorpark, Camarillo, and Chatsworth. The lot is in the Simi Town Center at 1555 Simi Town Center Way, Suite 420.

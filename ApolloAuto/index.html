Common questions

Used car buying — answered honestly

What buyers ask most before visiting. Plain answers, no dealer hype.

What makes Apollo Auto different from a chain dealership?

Tim Harmantzis, the owner, works directly with buyers at both lots. No call centers, no rotating sales staff, no pressure tactics. He explains financing clearly, goes through paperwork in plain English, and is reachable by phone or text the same day. Pricing is transparent — no add-on fees at signing.

Can I get a used car loan with bad credit?

Yes. We work with buyers across the full credit spectrum — bad credit, no credit, first-time buyers, and those rebuilding after repossession or bankruptcy. Most applicants who work with us find a financing option that fits. Contact Tim before visiting and he can give you a realistic picture of what to expect.

How quickly can I get pre-approved?

Often the same day. Reach out with basic details about your situation and Tim can point you toward what you may qualify for before you visit. Bringing ID and proof of income on the day speeds things up significantly.

Do you take trade-ins?

Yes, at both Simi Valley and El Monte. Trade-in value can go toward your down payment, reducing what you need to finance. Bring the vehicle — and the title if you have it — to either lot for a free appraisal. See our trade-in guide for what affects value.

What documents do I need to buy a used car in California?

You'll need a valid driver's license or state ID, proof of insurance (required before you drive off), and proof of income if financing. If trading in, bring the title. Our full checklist covers everything.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The price you agree to is the price you pay. There are no add-on fees, no surprise documentation charges, and no last-minute line items at signing. You can review redacted paperwork examples in our resources section before you visit.

Can I see the paperwork before I come in?

Yes. Our resources section includes redacted contract examples so you can understand exactly what you'll be signing. This is part of how we keep the buying process transparent and low-pressure.

What areas do you serve?

From Simi Valley: Thousand Oaks, Moorpark, Camarillo, Ventura, Chatsworth, and the San Fernando Valley. From El Monte: West Covina, Baldwin Park, Monterey Park, Temple City, Arcadia, Rosemead, and the San Gabriel Valley. Both locations see buyers from across Southern California.

Can I browse inventory before visiting?

Yes — Simi Valley at apolloauto-to.com, El Monte at apolloauto-em.com. Both listings are kept current. Message Tim about any specific vehicle and he can confirm availability, hold it for a test drive, or answer questions about it.

Is there a warranty on used cars?

Pre-owned vehicles are sold as-is, as is standard for independent used car dealers in California. Some vehicles may carry remaining manufacturer warranty — ask Tim about any specific car you're considering. We encourage buyers to read our inspection checklist and buyer guides before purchasing.

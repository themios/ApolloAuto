Call or text Tim at (805) 404-3873 (Simi) or (626) 215-0440 (El Monte).

Financing for all credit types. We believe reliable transportation should be accessible. Our financing options cover good credit, bad credit, low credit scores, first-time buyers, past repossession, bankruptcy, and more. Most customers who apply with us get financed. We accept trade-ins and offer transparent pricing with no add-on or hidden fees.

Areas we serve. In addition to Simi Valley and El Monte, we help buyers from Thousand Oaks, Moorpark, Camarillo, Ventura, the San Fernando Valley, San Gabriel Valley, and across Southern California.
